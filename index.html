About Aesthetic Steel Detailing Services

Aesthetic steel detailing combines structural integrity with visual appeal, ensuring that every steel component is crafted with precision and creativity. Using advanced software like Tekla Structures, the detailing process guarantees small, medium and large-scale structural elements meet the highest standards of accuracy and design.

By integrating aesthetic elements with structural detailing, projects benefit from enhanced functionality, reduced rework, and streamlined coordination from design to installation. Whether for architectural details or robust steel structures, these services bring both beauty and strength to any construction.

Precision Engineering

Our detailing ensures exact specifications and measurements for perfect fabrication and assembly.

3D Modeling

Advanced 3D models allow visualization of the entire structure before fabrication begins.

Collaborative Approach

We work closely with engineers, architects, and contractors to ensure seamless project execution.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive steel detailing services to meet the diverse needs of construction and fabrication projects.

Structural Steel Detailing

Comprehensive detailing of columns, beams, braces, connections, and other structural elements with detailed shop drawings and erection plans.

  • Beam and column details
  • Connection design and detailing
  • Anchor bolt and base plate plans

Miscellaneous Steel Detailing

Detailed drawings for stairs, railings, ladders, platforms, and other non-structural steel elements with precise fabrication specifications.

  • Stair and railing designs
  • Platform and walkway details
  • Architectural steel elements

3D Modeling & BIM

Creation of detailed 3D models and building information modeling for comprehensive visualization and clash detection.

  • Tekla Structures modeling
  • BIM coordination
  • Clash detection and resolution

Documentation & Reporting

Comprehensive documentation including material lists, bolt lists, and detailed reports for fabrication and erection.

  • Material take-offs and BOMs
  • Fabrication and erection drawings
  • Quality control documentation
