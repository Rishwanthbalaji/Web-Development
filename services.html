Our Services

At Aesthetic Steel Detailing Service, we provide specialized services that combine technical accuracy with visual appeal. Our comprehensive approach ensures that every steel element is meticulously detailed for optimal fabrication and installation.

Safety-Oriented Structural Planning

Throughout the design and construction phases, we reduce reliance on temporary bracing and shoring, while maintaining the highest standards of workplace safety.

Key Features

  • Safety-first approach to structural planning
  • Minimized temporary supports
  • Workplace safety compliance

Structural & Miscellaneous Steel Detailing

We deliver accurate fabrication and erection drawings, detailed material reports, NC1 files, and model exports to ensure efficient procurement, fabrication, and installation, while upholding the highest standards of quality assurance.

Key Features

  • Precise fabrication drawings
  • Comprehensive erection plans
  • Detailed material reporting
  • NC1 files for manufacturing

Estimate Models

We provide detailed structural steel 3D estimate models to support accurate budgeting, streamlined planning, and informed decision-making, ensuring efficiency and adherence to the highest quality standards throughout the project lifecycle.

Key Features

  • Accurate 3D estimate models
  • Precise budgeting support
  • Streamlined project planning

Tekla API Development

Key Features

  • Workflow automation
  • Productivity enhancements
  • Integration with other systems
